It was about 6:30 p.m. on Thursday, April 18, 2024.
Members of the Youth Violence Strike Force were on patrol.
We were in the area of 32 Forest Street.
We observed a group congregating.
We exited our cruiser.
We intended to engage in conversation with the group.
We approached the group.
I observed one individual blading his body away from us.
The individual was later identified as Sean Blackwell.
I observed a weighted object weighing down the right side of the individual's jacket.
We conducted a pat frisk of Blackwell.
We recovered a firearm from Blackwell's right jacket pocket.
The recovered weapon was determined to be a Taurus firearm.
The firearm contained one round in the chamber.
The firearm contained five rounds in the magazine.
Blackwell is 18 years old.
Blackwell is of Roxbury.
Blackwell was placed under arrest.
Blackwell was charged with unlawful possession of a firearm.
Blackwell was charged with unlawful possession of ammunition.
Blackwell stated that he lived at 62 Forest Street.
We identified a 13-year-old male in the group.
The 13-year-old male was identified as Rony Almanza.
We spoke with Rony Almanza.
Almanza was observed wearing plastic gloves.
Almanza claimed he wore the gloves because he was cold.
We identified another individual as Uzi.
An officer read the individual his Miranda rights.
The individual was advised of the right to remain silent.
The individual was advised that anything he said may be used against him in a court of law.
The individual was advised of the right to consult with a lawyer before answering any questions.
The individual was advised of the right to have a lawyer present during questioning.
The individual was advised that a lawyer would be provided by the Commonwealth without cost if he could not afford one.
The individual was asked if he understood these rights.
The individual responded, "No."
An officer issued a demand for the individual's license to carry.
