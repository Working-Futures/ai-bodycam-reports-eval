Officers assigned to District A7, East Boston, responded to the area of 293 Border Street.
The officers responded at about 4:14 p.m. on Tuesday, March 26, 2024.
The response was for a report of an armed robbery.
Officers were advised that a suspect brandished a knife.
Officers were advised that a suspect hit the victim with a rock.
Officers were advised that a suspect began kicking the victim in the head.
The suspect allegedly robbed the victim of about $500.
Officers broadcasted a description of the suspect.
Officers located an individual matching the description.
The individual was congregating with a group behind the building.
Officers made contact with the suspect.
Officers ordered the suspect to take his hands out of his pockets multiple times.
The suspect was identified as Lennon Esteban Rios Ramos.
Rios Ramos stated, "I'm not a criminal."
Officers detained Rios Ramos.
Officers conducted a pat frisk of Rios Ramos.
Officers asked Rios Ramos if he had anything sharp or dangerous.
Rios Ramos replied, "No."
Officers recovered a black folding knife from Rios Ramos during the search.
Officers recovered a medium-sized rock from Rios Ramos during the search.
Officers recovered a screwdriver from Rios Ramos during the search.
Officers questioned Rios Ramos about the items.
Rios Ramos stated he uses the items for his work in the north.
Rios Ramos stated he works with the Capones family.
Officers noted that Rios Ramos did not have any money on his person.
Officers coordinated via radio regarding a viewing of the suspect.
Rios Ramos was placed under arrest.
Rios Ramos was charged with armed robbery.
Rios Ramos was charged with assault and battery.
Rios Ramos was charged with assault and battery with a dangerous weapon (shod foot).
Rios Ramos was charged with assault with a dangerous weapon (screwdriver).
Rios Ramos was charged with assault with a dangerous weapon (rock).
