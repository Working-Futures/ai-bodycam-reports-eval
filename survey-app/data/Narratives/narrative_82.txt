On Thursday, March 21, 2024, at approximately 10:21 a.m., officers assigned to District B-2, Roxbury, responded to the Burger King located at 100 Washington Street in Dorchester regarding reports of an individual wielding a knife. The witness described the weapon as a "little head knife."
