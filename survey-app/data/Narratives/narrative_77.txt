Narrative: At about 4:14 p.m. on Tuesday, March 26, 2024, officers assigned to District A7, East Boston, responded to the area of 293 Border Street for a report of an armed robbery. Officers were advised that a suspect brandished a knife, hit the victim with a rock, and began kicking the victim in the head. The suspect then allegedly robbed the victim of about $500. Officers broadcasted a description of the suspect and subsequently located an individual matching the description congregating with a group behind the building.

Upon making contact, officers ordered the suspect to take his hands out of his pockets multiple times. The suspect, identified as Lennon Esteban Rios Ramos, stated, "I'm not a criminal." Officers detained Rios Ramos and conducted a pat frisk. When asked if he had anything sharp or dangerous, Rios Ramos replied, "No." During the search, officers recovered a black folding knife, a medium-sized rock, and a screwdriver from Rios Ramos. When questioned about the items, Rios Ramos stated he uses them for his work in the north with the Capones family. Officers also noted that Rios Ramos did not have any money on his person.

Officers coordinated via radio regarding a viewing of the suspect. Rios Ramos was subsequently placed under arrest and charged with armed robbery, assault and battery, assault and battery with a dangerous weapon (shod foot), assault with a dangerous weapon (screwdriver), and assault with a dangerous weapon (rock).
